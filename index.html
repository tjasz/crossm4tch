CROSSM4TCH

Match tiles by color or number in order to claim a winning sequence.

Last Tile:

To start: tap any tile on the outer ring of the game board to claim it.

Players alternate turns claiming tiles.

Only tiles that match either the color or the numeral of the previous tile can be claimed.

To win: claim either a full row, column, diagonal, or 2x2 square of tiles.

It is also possible to win by claiming a tile which has no available matching tiles.
